About leveuro.com

leveuro.com is a free, mobile-friendly currency conversion tool built for fast and accurate conversion between the Bulgarian Lev (BGN) and the Euro (EUR).

The exchange rate used is the official fixed rate set by the Bulgarian National Bank: 1 EUR = 1.95583 BGN.

This tool is ideal for:

  • Travelers in Bulgaria
  • Freelancers and businesses handling BGN/EUR payments
  • Everyday users looking for a quick conversion reference
