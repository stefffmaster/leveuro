About leveuro.com

leveuro.com is a free, mobile-optimized currency converter created to support Bulgarians as the country prepares to join the eurozone on January 1, 2026.

It uses the official fixed exchange rate of 1 EUR = 1.95583 BGN, allowing anyone to instantly convert between Bulgarian Lev (BGN) and Euro (EUR).

Who is it for?

  • People preparing for the Bulgarian euro transition
  • Freelancers, businesses, travelers handling BGN/EUR
  • Anyone needing a clean, instant currency reference
