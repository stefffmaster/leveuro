Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is the exchange rate from Euro to Lev?

The official fixed rate is 1 EUR = 1.95583 BGN. This rate has remained stable due to Bulgaria's currency board system.

Is the conversion rate live?

No. The tool uses the fixed rate published by the Bulgarian National Bank, which rarely changes.

Can I convert Lev to Euro and vice versa?

Yes. You can input a value in either currency and see the converted value instantly.

Absolutely. leveuro.com is a completely free tool with no registration.
