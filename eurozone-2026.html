Bulgaria's Eurozone Entry in 2026: What You Need to Know

On January 1, 2026, Bulgaria is expected to adopt the euro as its official currency, replacing the lev (BGN). This marks a major financial milestone for the country.

What changes on this date?

  • All BGN accounts will convert to EUR automatically
  • Prices will be shown in both currencies during the transition period
  • Cash in lev can be exchanged at no cost for a limited period
