Understanding the Fixed Lev ↔ Euro Exchange Rate

The lev is pegged to the euro under a currency board arrangement, and the fixed rate is 1 EUR = 1.95583 BGN. This rate has remained stable for years.

Why is the rate fixed?

The fixed rate helps keep inflation and currency volatility low in Bulgaria as it transitions to the euro.

Will the rate change in 2026?

No — the same rate will be used when Bulgaria enters the eurozone.
